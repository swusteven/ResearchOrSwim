About:

ResearchOrSwim simplifies and streamlines your investment research process. It's just simple as just one click away.


ReserchOrSwim is a stock investing analytics dashboard based on price momemtum, earning fundamentals and Wallstreet sentiments. The dashboard provides a single-page view to users that includes the following:

  • 1-yr Historical price chart with a 30-days moving average for momentum investing.
  • Yearly earnings for fundamentals investing.
  • Wallstreet sentiment based on analysts recommendations.

Instructions:

Enter the stock symbol in the upper left search field. ResearchOrSwim will do all the works for you

Analytics result will display in the upper right section: Recommended or Not Recommended

Search again if you want to check out another company


Analytics and Rules:

In addition, ResearchOrSwim performs analysis based on below investment principles/rules and then recommend a 'buy' only if it passed all test levels.

Buy recommendation logic:

  • Price Momentum: passed only if price moving average from last 30 days is increasing.
  • Company Earnings: passed only if earning is positive and YoY growth in the last two years was 10% or more.
  • Analyst Recommendation: passed only if 50% of the analysts recommended Strong Buy or Buy.

This provides great benefits to investors who implement investment strategies strictly based on the defined rules,rather than sentiments.
