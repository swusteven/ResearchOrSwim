About:

ReserchOrSwim is a stock investing analytics dashboard based on price momentum, earning fundamentals and Wallstreet sentiments. It simplified your investment research process as it's just simple as just one click away and included buy/sell recommendations.


Instructions:

  • Enter the stock symbol in the upper left search field
  • Analytics result will display in the upper right section: Recommended or Not Recommended
  • Search again if you want to check out another company

Note:

This is a JavaScript and D3.js project to strengthen my technical skills. Sandbox environment data are being used therefore contents in this site is for illustration purposes.
