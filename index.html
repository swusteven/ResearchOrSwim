3 Investing Principles:

ReserchOrSwim is a stock investing analytics dashboard based on price momentum, earning fundamentals and Wall Street sentiments. The dashboard consists of the following features:

  • 1-year historical price chart with a 30-days moving average to gauge price momentum.
  • Yearly earnings to gauge earning strength.
  • Wall Street recommendations to gauge sentiments.
  • ResearchOrSwim Recommendations.

ResearchOrSwim Recommendations:

In addition, ResearchOrSwim performs in-depth analysis based on below investing principles and rules. A 'buy' recommendation only if that specific stock passed ALL of the following 3 investing principles.

  • Price Momentum: passed only if price moving average from last 30 days is increasing.
  • Company Earnings: passed only if earning is positive in the last two years and YoY growth was 10% or more.
  • Wall Street Analysts Recommendations: passed only if 50% of the overall analysts recommended a Strong Buy or Buy.
