About:

ReserchOrSwim is a stock investing analytics dashboard based on 3 investing principals - price momentum, earning fundamentals and Wall Street sentiments. It simplified your investment research process as it's just simple as just one click away.


In addition, it included buy/sell analysis based on the aforementioned investing principals.


Instructions:

  • Review analytics results
  • Search again if you want to check out another company

3 Investing Principals :

ReserchOrSwim is a stock investing analytics dashboard based on price momentum, earning fundamentals and Wall Street sentiments:

  • 1-year historical price chart with a 30-days moving average to gauge price momentum.
  • Yearly earnings to gauge earning strength.
  • Wall Street recommendations to gauge sentiments.

ResearchOrSwim Recommendations:

In addition, ResearchOrSwim performs in-depth analysis based on below investing principals and rules. A 'buy' recommendation only if that specific stock passed ALL of the below 3 investing principals.

  • Price Momentum: passed only if price moving average from last 30 days is increasing.
  • Company Earnings: passed only if earning is positive in the last two years and YoY growth was 10% or more.
  • Wall Street Analysts Recommendations: passed only if 50% of the analysts recommended Strong Buy or Buy.
